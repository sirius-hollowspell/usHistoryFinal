Michelle Obama

Started “Let’s Move!” program to end childhood obesity. Lead the “Reach Higher Initiative” to help students understand job opportunities and education and skills they need for those jobs

Hillary Rodham Clinton

Graduate of Yale Law School. Advised the Children’s Defense Fund. Joined impeachment inquiry staff advising the Judiciary Committee of the House of Representatives. Served as Arkansas’s First Lady for 12 years. Chaired Task Force on National Health Care Reform. Leading advocate for expanding health insurance coverage and ensured children were properly immunized. Elected United States Senator from New York. She is the first First Lady elected to the United States Senate and the first woman elected statewide in New York.
